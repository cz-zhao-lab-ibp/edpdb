Convert between rhombohefral and hexagonal cells
Rhombohedral cell parameters:
cell dimensions (a=b=c) , angles (alpha=beta=gamma)

Hexagonal cell parameters:
cell dimensions (a=b) (c) , angles (alpha=beta=90, gamma=120)

Reindex from an R-cell (hkl) to a hexagonal cell (HKL):

  • H= h-k
  • K= k-l
  • L= h+k+l

Conditions limiting possible reflections:

  • -H+K+L= 3n
